Machine Vision

Can a machine touch the human soul? In Machine Vision, artificial intelligence examines the world through diverse imagery, offering profound interpretations that mirror the complexities of human thought. This project invites us to question not only what machines can perceive, but whether they can truly grasp the depth of our emotions, stories, and essence.
